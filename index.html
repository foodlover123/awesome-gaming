All about Siberian Huskies

They are medium sized,have a dense-coat, is a working dog breed that originated in north-eastern Siberia

Life span - 12 – 14 years

They are outgoing, alert, gentle, friendly, intelligent

They can come in Black, White, Red, Grey, Splash, Sable, Black , Tan, Copper, Brown, Black , White, Silver, Agouti, Piebald

They have a huge amount of energy.

All About Pembroke Welsh Corgi

They are a cattle herding dog breed which originated in Pembrokeshire, Wales

They are Outgoing, Playful, Bold, Protective, Tenacious, Friendly

They come in Red, Fawn, Sable, Black , Tan, Blue, Black , White

Life span - 12 – 14 years
