Some attributes are reliable, friendly, kind, confident, trustworthy, Intelligent

They can come in Light Golden, Golden, Dark Golden, Cream

Height - Female: 20–22 inches (51–56 cm), Male: 22–24 inches (56–61 cm)

All about Siberian Huskies(they are so friendly)

They are outgoing, alert, gentle, friendly, intelligent

Life span - 12 – 14 years

They are medium sized,have a dense-coat, is a working dog breed that originated in north-eastern Siberia

They can come in Black, White, Red, Grey, Splash, Sable, Black , Tan, Copper, Brown, Black , White, Silver, Agouti, Piebald

They have a huge amount of energy.

height Female: 20–22 inches (50–56 cm), Male: 21–24 inches (54–60 cm)

weight Female: 35–51 lbs (16–23 kg), Male: 44–60 lbs (20–27 kg)

All About Pembroke Welsh Corgi(so cute)

They are Outgoing, Playful, Bold, Protective, Tenacious, Friendly

They are a cattle herding dog breed which originated in Pembrokeshire, Wales

They come in Red, Fawn, Sable, Black , Tan, Blue, Black , White

Life span - 12 – 14 years

Weight - Male: 22–26 lbs (10–12 kg), Female: 22–24 lbs (10–11 kg)

Height - Male: 10–12 inches (25–30 cm), Female: 10–12 inches (25–30 cm)

All about Labrador Retrievers(fluffy and calm)

Attributes: Outgoing, Even Tempered, Gentle, Agile, Kind, Intelligent, Trusting

Life span: 10 – 12 years

They can come in black, yellow, chocolate

Labrador is one of the most popular breeds of dog in the United Kingdom and the United States.

Coat-Smooth, short, dense and straight

Weight - Male: 64–79 lbs (29–36 kg), Female: 55–71 lbs (25–32 kg)

Height - Male: 22–24 inches (57–62 cm), Female: 22–24 inches (55–60 cm)
